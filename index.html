🦴 Adopt, don't shop! 🦴

Your best friend is waiting for you at the shelter...

With over 200,000 dogs euthanised throughout Australia each year in shelters, the message of ‘Adopt, Don’t Shop’ is literally saving lives each day.

Always Adopt, Don’t Shop.
Ensure your companion animals are desexed, as well as registered and microchipped so they can be returned to you if they ever are lost. And remember, an animal is a friend for life!
